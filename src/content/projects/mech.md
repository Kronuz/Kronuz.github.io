Mech is a command-line tool for managing virtual machines, in the spirit of Vagrant but
aimed at VMware (Fusion and Workstation) and free to use. Vagrant's VMware support lived
behind a paid plugin; mech does the same job, scriptably and for nothing. Spin a box up,
snapshot it, share it, and tear it down, all from a small CLI.

I didn't start this one. Kevin Chung wrote the original. I came in later and ended up the
top contributor, with more than twice the commits, and drove its branding and the move into
its own GitHub organization. It's the cleanest example of a move I keep making: take a
useful project, give it a real identity, stand up an org, and transfer it in so it can grow
past any one person's account. The same thing happened with
[SublimeLinter](/projects/sublimelinter/) and
[SublimeCodeIntel](/projects/sublimecodeintel/).
